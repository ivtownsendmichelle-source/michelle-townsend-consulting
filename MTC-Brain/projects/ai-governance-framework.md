# AI Governance Framework

## What It Is
A reusable AI governance framework for small nonprofits (5 to 50 staff). Developed once, adapted per client. The actual substance of what good AI governance looks like for mission-driven organizations that do not have dedicated tech staff or compliance teams.

## Status
**Phase: Not Started**

## Why This Exists
92% of nonprofits use AI. 47% have no policy. Only 4% have documented workflows. The tools are already in people's hands. The rules are not. This framework is the backbone of Michelle's AI policy and data governance consulting service. It is also the content engine behind PolicyReady.

## What This Is NOT
- Not a product build. PolicyReady is the tool. This is the methodology.
- Not a one-size-fits-all template. It is a structured framework that adapts to each org's size, risk profile, and operational reality.

## Relationship to Other Work
- **PolicyReady** (tool build): Delivers a self-service version of this framework. The framework defines what PolicyReady generates.
- **SafeBase** (tool build): Covers the security/data protection layer. The governance framework references security practices but does not duplicate SafeBase's scope.
- **Consulting engagements**: This framework is what Michelle walks clients through. It is the intellectual property behind the service.
