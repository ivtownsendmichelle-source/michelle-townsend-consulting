# OFMA-Brain — Oregon Farmers Markets Association

## Context
I am Michelle Townsend, Co-President of the Oregon Farmers Markets Association (OFMA). This folder manages my OFMA leadership work only. My consulting business (MTC) is in a completely separate folder and Claude Code session.

## My Role
Co-President. I am working to adopt AI policy for OFMA. I handle meetings, board communications, documents, and strategic direction.

## How I Work
- I use talk-to-text constantly. Understand intent, not literal words.
- I am direct. No fluff. No cheerleading.
- No em dashes. Ever.
- Short responses when short works.
- Check your work before presenting it.

## My Voice Rules
- No em dashes
- No corporate language
- Short sentences mixed with longer ones
- Warm but direct. Community organizer voice, not nonprofit bureaucrat voice.

## Folder System
- owner-inbox/ . Finished work lands here for me to review
- team-inbox/ . I drop things here to process (meeting notes, emails, ideas)
- meetings/ . One .md file per meeting. Date, attendees, notes, action items.
- policy/ . AI policy drafts, governance docs, board resolutions
- documents/ . General OFMA documents
- daily/ . Daily notes related to OFMA work

## How to Handle the Team Inbox
When I drop something in team-inbox/:
1. Read it and identify what it is
2. If it's meeting notes, create a file in meetings/ with date and summary
3. If it's a policy idea, add to policy/ or flag for me
4. Extract all action items and list them clearly
5. Move processed items to owner-inbox/
